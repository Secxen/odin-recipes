Yaki Onigiri

Description

Don't be fooled, these are NOT jelly filled donuts (thanks localized ver of Pokemon.) Yaki in Japanese means grilled. Onigiri are rice balls. Thus: Grilled Riceballs.

This is a really easy yet fun take on a very convient Japanese snack. I like to pack these for long trips to the zoo or as just to whip up as a fun afternoon snack for my family.

This recipe does require you to handle rice with your hands. To avoid a sticky mess, be sure to wet your hands before handling the cooked rice.

Prep Time:15 - 20 minutes

Cook Time:5 - 10 minutes

Total Time:20 - 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice (preferrably left out to cool so you don't burn your hands)
  • Your choice of filling (I typically use salmon flakes mixed with japanese mayo or japanese sour plums
  • Furikake (or crushed seaweed and sesame seeds if you don't have any)
  • 1/3 cup Soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. To prep your onigiri you must have the follower: one plate for the molding area, one plate for the setting area, one plate for your rice.

  2. With wet hands (I also like to salt them but that's up to you), grab a handful or rice and mold it into a ball.

    (the trick to making the infamous triangle shape is by using bent hands (fingers bent at the base) and molding the rice to the shape of your hands)

    If the concept is too difficult, you can look up tutorials on youtube!

  3. Press the center of the rice ball to make a lil bowl like space. Fill this with your choice of filling.
  4. Cover the filling with a bit more rice and reform the shape of your onigiri. Repeat these steps until you have your desired amount of onigiri.
  5. Make your glaze! Combine your soy sauce and sugar in a bowl, adjust to taste.
  6. Warm up a pan on medium low heat. Once warm, brush your glaze over the face of your onigiri and place it into the pan.
  7. Grill the onigiri for 1-3 minutes. Glaze the side facing up before flipping and grilling for another 1-2 minutes.
  8. Repeat until all of your onigiri have been grilled. Garnish with furikake and enjoy.
